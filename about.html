About me 😊

Hi there, I am Sahil. I am a creative, logical and detail-oriented frontend developer working remotely at Vasitum. My passion for web development started by writing Hello World in index.html. Since then, I have strived to learn new things and enhance my skills.

Apart from work I love watching anime, playing chess, reading fiction and hiking.
