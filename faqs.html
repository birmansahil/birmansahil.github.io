Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ page! Here, you will find answers to common questions about Humber Merch, from our product offerings and submission process to our mission and values. If you have any additional questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to our team. We are here to help and ensure you have a seamless and enjoyable experience with us.

Q. What is Humber Merch?

Humber Merch is a campus store dedicated to showcasing and selling student-created products. From unique artwork and stylish apparel to innovative crafts, we provide a platform for students to display their talents and reach a wider audience.

Q. How can I submit my product for consideration?

To submit your product, fill out our online application form and provide details about your creation, including a description, images, and any relevant information. Our team will review your submission and get in touch with you if your product meets our criteria.

Q. What criteria do you use to approve products?

We look for products that reflect creativity, quality, and originality. Each product must meet our standards for craftsmanship and presentation. We also consider how well the product aligns with our mission to support and showcase student talent.

Q. How can I get involved with Humber Merch?

There are various ways to get involved, including submitting your own creations, volunteering, or applying for job opportunities. Visit our Careers page for more information on how to join our team or contribute to our mission.

Q. What should I do if I have more questions?

If you have any additional questions or need further assistance, feel free to contact us query@humbermerch.ca. Our team is here to help and ensure you have a great experience with Humber Merch.
